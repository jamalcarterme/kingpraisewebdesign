The Challenge

Common problems real estate agencies face online

Listings are hard to browse

Static PDF flyers or unsearchable pages frustrate buyers, who leave for a site with proper filters and photos.

Leads aren't captured

Without inquiry forms tied to specific listings, agents lose track of who's interested in which property.

Low visibility for local property searches

Agencies without SEO miss out on buyers actively searching “property for sale in Lagos” and similar terms.
